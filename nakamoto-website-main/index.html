What is Yama.

Yama is an advanced ATS (Applicant Tracking System) and resume generation product designed to streamline and optimize the hiring process. Utilizing cutting-edge AI technology, Yama offers a comprehensive suite of features tailored to meet the needs of students, companies, and recruiters

Resume Generation:

Yama generates tailored resumes by analyzing job descriptions and existing resumes, ensuring that candidate presentations align perfectly with job requirements.

ATS Scoring:

Provides accurate ATS scores for resumes based on job descriptions, helping recruiters quickly identify the best candidates for each role

One-Stop Hiring:

Yama serves as a comprehensive destination for all your hiring needs, from resume generation to candidate evaluation and selection.

Continuous Learning:

Yama evolves with every interaction, refining its algorithms to deliver increasingly precise and valuable insights over time.

What is ATS

An ATS helps companies organize candidates hiring and recruitment purposes. These systems allow businesses of all sizes and across industries to collect candidate information, organize prospects based on experience and skill set, and filter applicants.

How our Checker Can help

We offer the ability to customize Yama's models based on your data, ensuring that our solutions meet the specific needs and nuances of your organization. We also make it easy integrate Yama with existing HR systems and job portals, ensuring a cohesive and efficient recruitment workflow.
